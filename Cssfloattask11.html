Apple Inc. is a global technology leader renowned for its innovation and design excellence. Founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976, the company revolutionized personal computing with the Apple I and II. Today, Apple is celebrated for its iconic products, including the iPhone, iPad, and MacBook. With a focus on sleek aesthetics and user-friendly interfaces, Apple continues to drive technological advancement and shape the future of digital experiences.
